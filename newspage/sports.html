Tanzania stars at AFCON 2025

Tanzania played Nigeria in the 2025 Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) opening match but lost 2–1 in a competitive encounter in Fès, Morocco. Nigeria’s Semi Ajayi and Ademola Lookman scored, while Tanzania’s Charles M’Mombwa netted their goal.

The national team includes key players with international experience like Tarryn Allarakhia and Haji Mnoga, who represent Tanzania at this major continental tournament.

AFCON 2025 is shaping up as a major showcase for African football, with top teams and players competing across Morocco
